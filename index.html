This calculator is a tool to determine the day when the Watt Traders in the Sword & Shield Wild Area will have a specific Technical Record (TR) or Poké Ball (ball) for purchase. Enter in the location of a trader, the first TR he has for purchase, and your desired TR/ball. The calculator will tell you which trader will have your desired TR/ball and how many days into the future it will take for it to appear.


Usage of this tool is as follows:

  1. Enter the location of your reference Watt Trader. There are 7 in the Wild Area: Meetup Spot, East Lake Axewell, Dappled Grove, Giant's Seat, Bridge Field, Hammerlocke Hills, and Giant's Cap.
  2. Enter the first TR your reference Watt Trader is selling. This is the one listed first in his offerings, not necessarily the lowest TR number. You can type in the box to filter the options.
  3. Enter the TR you'd like the purchase. You can type in the box to filter the options.
  4. Alternatively to step 3, you can select the Poké Ball you'd like to purcase from the appropriate dropdown.
  5. Click the button to search for either your desired TR or your desired ball. The buttons are labeled appropriately.

The tool will output how many days into the future your desired TR/ball will be sold and at which location. "After 0 day(s)..." refers to today. "After 1 day(s)..." refers to tomorrow. "After 2 day(s)..." is the day after tomorrow. And so forth. For your convenience a picture of the location of the Watt Trader is included as well.


The time needed to wait for your desired TR/ball can be skipped by using a glitch in the Sword & Shield games. This glitch allows you to change the clock on your Switch system settings while making the game think a full day has passed. The steps are as follows:

  1. Find an active den in the Wild Area. It is recommended to use a den activated by a Wishing Piece if you need to skip more than 1 day for your desired TR/ball.
  2. Click A on the den, and begin searching for partners ("Invite others")
  3. While searching, press the home button and navigate to the date and time settings (System Settings > System > Date and Time)
  4. Turn off Synchronize Clock via Internet and advance the day
  5. Click "OK" to confirm the date change
  6. Return to the game and back out of the den (return to the overworld)
  7. Repeat until you have skipped the correct number of days
